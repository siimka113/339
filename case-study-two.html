SFU Blueprint Website

Enhancing club engagement by designing a responsive website, fostering inclusivity and enthusiasm among potential members and partners.

Fall 2023 - SPRING 2024

INTRO

The SFU Blueprint website serves as an access point for student club involvement, non-profits, and potential sponsors. My role was to design a responsive website that meets accessibility standards, while also incorporating unique branding and art direction.

I became involved with SFU Blueprint in September 2023, and have been designing the club's website until recently, in collaboration with developers. The site's development is currently ongoing!

OVERVIEW

What is Blueprint?

Blueprint is a team of students who help non-profits by providing technology solutions.

Established at Simon Fraser University in 2023, the club is part of a larger collective of chapters spread across North American Universities, with its origins in UC Berkeley. As it's a new team, reaching as many people as possible from the outset is essential.


The Design Challenge

Design an engaging website that both informs and encourages visitors to get involved. The design should balance a unique UI/UX and art direction specific to SFU, while maintaining the overarching Blueprint branding.


FINAL SITE

Desktop Experience


Mobile Experience

In the transition from desktop to mobile interface for the website, special attention was paid to ensuring a seamless user experience across devices. Navigation was simplified for the mobile interface, providing users with easy access to key sections of the portfolio. Furthermore, visual elements were optimized for mobile viewing, ensuring clear and engaging presentation even on smaller screens.



CONCLUSION

Designing for the web means letting go of pixel-perfect perfection, instead prioritizing responsiveness and accessibility.

Creating responsive layouts was more time-consuming than I anticipated. I discovered that the main task of web designers is not to achieve perfection like in print design, but to ensure that the content templates are as flexible as possible. This is a skill I'm still learning. However, this project was a valuable first step towards mastering it.
